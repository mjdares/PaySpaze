Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create a new event and start selling tickets on PaySpaze?

Getting started with PaySpaze is easy! Simply create a new event on our platform and start customizing it to make it unique. You can add information about the event, set up ticket prices, and even upload promotional images or videos. Once you’re done, you’ll be able to start selling tickets and see them come in from all over the world. So don't wait any longer - create an event now and take your business to the next level with PaySpaze!

What kind of events can I host and sell tickets for on the platform?

PaySpaze allows you to create and sell tickets for any type of event: from music concerts to conferences, art expositions, poetry readings, sports events and much more. We also offer customizable features like options to set up payment gateways or add additional services. With our service, you can make sure your event is organized in the most efficient way possible.

Is it easy to customize my event page and ticket design with PaySpaze?

Absolutely! With PaySpaze you have the ability to customize your event page easily. We also provide a library of templates that you can modify to suit your own unique style.

Can I track sales data and attendees through the PaySpaze dashboard?

Absolutely! With PaySpaze, you will get a comprehensive breakdown of your event’s sales and attendees. You can manage ticketing, view sales data, and much more from the dashboard. This way, you can keep track of everything related to your event in one place!
